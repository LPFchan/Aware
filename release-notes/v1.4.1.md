## New in 1.4.1

- Screensaver no longer interrupts presence detection — when a face is detected, both the display-sleep and screensaver idle timers are reset each polling tick, so the screensaver only activates when Aware genuinely stops seeing you
- Aware now skips the camera check when another app (e.g. a browser playing a video) is already holding a sleep assertion, and shows **"Kept awake by another app"** status
